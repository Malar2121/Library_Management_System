ABOUT US

  • SLIIT maintains three libraries at Metro, Malabe campuses and the Matara centre. Each holds a latest collection of books and periodicals, particularly in the field of Information Technology, Information Systems & Management, Computer Systems & Networking, and Electronic Engineering. The library of the Malabe Campus acts as the main resource center through which all library development activities are coordinated. SLIIT libraries are constantly updated to accommodate resource materials relating to new developments in Information and Communication Technology and other subjects relating to the core teaching areas. The library is being augmented continuously to cater to the increasing number of students and for the propagation of knowledge.
